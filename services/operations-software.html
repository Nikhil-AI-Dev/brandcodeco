The Spreadsheet Problem

Spreadsheets are where operations go to die slowly. The problems are predictable:

  • Version chaos — Three people editing the same file means three different realities
  • No real-time visibility — By the time you reconcile, the damage is done
  • Manual errors compound — One wrong cell cascades through every formula that depends on it
  • No accountability — Spreadsheets don't log who changed what, or when
  • Can't alert — A spreadsheet doesn't ping you when something goes wrong

Purpose-built operations software solves all five. It's not about being fancy — it's about not losing money to problems you could've caught in real time.
