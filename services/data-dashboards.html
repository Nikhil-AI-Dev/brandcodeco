Why Not Just Use Tableau or Power BI?

Off-the-shelf BI tools are powerful. They're also:

  • Expensive per seat — $70+/user/month adds up fast when you want the whole team to have access
  • Generic — You get a tool that can show anything, which means it shows nothing well by default. Customization takes weeks of consultant time.
  • Disconnected — Dashboards live in a separate app your team has to remember to check. Custom dashboards can live inside the tools they already use.
  • Rigid on data sources — Connecting non-standard APIs, IoT devices, or custom databases often requires expensive middleware.

A custom dashboard costs less than a year of per-seat BI licensing for most mid-market teams — and it shows exactly what matters, nothing more.
